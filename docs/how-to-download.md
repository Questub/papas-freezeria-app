The Papa's Freezeria app lets players create delicious frozen treats while managing a busy ice cream shop.

## Option 1 — Browser / Web App

### Step 1 — Access the Game
1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Go to the official Papa's Freezeria website or a trusted game platform that hosts the app.
3. Click on the "Play Now" button to launch the game.

### Step 2 — Create an Account (Optional)
1. If prompted, create a free account to save your progress.
2. Fill in the required information such as email and password.
3. Confirm your account via the email link if necessary.

### Step 3 — Start Playing
1. Choose your character and customize your shop settings.
2. Follow the on-screen tutorial to learn the game mechanics.
3. Begin managing your ice cream shop and serving customers!

## Option 2 — Desktop App

### Step 1 — Download the App
1. Visit a reputable app store or the official Papa's Freezeria website.
2. Locate the download link for the desktop version of the app.
3. Click on the link to start the download process.

### Step 2 — Install the App
1. Once the download is complete, locate the installer file on your computer.
2. Double-click the file to initiate the installation.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.

### Step 3 — Launch the App
1. Find the Papa's Freezeria app icon on your desktop or in your applications folder.
2. Double-click the icon to open the app.
3. Create an account or log in if you already have one, then start playing!
